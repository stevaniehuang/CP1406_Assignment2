What is Fish-Tastic?!

Founded in 2013 by Mr K.H.Chan, the co-sponsor of Singapore Sea Aquarium.

FISH~TASTIC sells a variety of fishes with prices ranging from affordable to luxurious. We also carries products such as accessories and fish foods of many well-known brands,
such as Hikari, Tropical, and Rio.

We have trading partners worldwide, whom we have business dealings with in countries all over the world. We continue to explore and look for trading partners in other parts of the world, so as to acquire more products to develop our farm into a one-stop shopping paradise for all fish lovers.

Come and visit our store to make your marine aquarium beautiful and pristine. We are here to share our knowledge and make your hobby as easy as possible. We believe that it is truly a privilege to be able to look at a part of the ocean everyday.

If you have any questions, please feel free to contact us by phone or email.
